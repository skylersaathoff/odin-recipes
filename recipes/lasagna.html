Lasagna

Discription

This is a Lasagna recipe. It is very good and everyone will love it alot!

Ingredients

  • meat
  • onion and garlic
  • tomato products
  • sugar
  • spces and seasonings
  • lasagna noodles
  • cheese
  • egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving
