Enchaladas recipe

Discription

This is describing enchaladas. I love them!

Ingredients

  • union
  • enchalada sauce
  • tortilla
  • meat
  • taco seasoning

Steps

  1. Cut unions and cook in pan
  2. add meat and seasoning
  3. cook for ten minutes
  4. roll tortillas with cooked meat
  5. place in oven for 20 min
  6. take out of oven and serve
